The Technical Process

From AI conceptualization to final physical prototype.

1. Prompt Engineering

Crafting detailed master prompts for ChatGPT/Midjourney to generate layout bases, typography variations, and herbal assets.

2. Vector Tracing

Importing generated flat assets into Adobe Illustrator. Using Image Trace and pen tool to clean up logos, badges, and flavor icons into scalable vectors.

3. Dieline Application

Setting up exact print measurements. Applying the vector assets over the designated dielines for the box, tube wrapper, and sachet.

4. Physical Prototyping

Sanding and spray-painting blank physical bottles with Plastic Primer and Mint Green acrylic. Applying printed transparent sticker labels.
