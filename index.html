THE NEW ERA OF
HERBAL CARE.

Transforming a traditional tooth powder into a premium, Gen Z-focused global oral care ecosystem. Complete redesign featuring physical packaging and visual assets.

The Strategy Shift

Moving away from the chemical mass market aesthetic to a minimalist, trustworthy, and organic visual language.

Organic Visuals

Replacing harsh yellows with soothing mint greens, teals, and charcoal to emphasize the 99.9% herbal and natural ingredients.

Product Expansion

Evolving from a single powder tin to a complete oral care family: Tubes, On-the-go Sachets, and Premium Mouthwash.

Target Audience

Specifically designed for Gen Z and Millennials who value transparency, aesthetic bathroom shelf appeal, and organic formulas.

The Technical Process

From conceptualization to final physical prototype creation.

1. Asset Generation

Crafting detailed master concepts for layout bases, typography variations, and high-quality herbal assets.

2. Vector Tracing & Editing

Importing flat assets into Adobe Illustrator. Using Image Trace and the pen tool to clean up logos and flavor icons into scalable vectors.

3. Dieline Application

Setting up exact print measurements. Applying the vector assets over the designated dielines for the packaging.

4. Physical Prototyping

Sanding and spray-painting blank physical bottles with Plastic Primer and Acrylic paint. Applying custom printed transparent sticker labels.
